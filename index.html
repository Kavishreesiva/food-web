Kitchen Tips

Cooking Tips for Beginners

Master your kitchen with these professional secrets

🥗

1. Prep First

Read recipes fully and chop all ingredients before starting (Mise en place) to stay stress-free.

🧂

2. Season Smart

Season gradually and taste frequently. Adjust salt and acidity (lemon/vinegar) for perfect balance.

🔥

3. Master Heat

High: Searing. Medium: Regular cooking. Low: Simmering. Right heat is the secret to great taste.

🍲

4. Core Skills

Focus on mastering Sautéing, Boiling, Roasting, and Frying to cook almost anything.

🧼

5. Clean & Go

Clean as you cook. Keep your workspace organized to save massive time and effort later.

⭐

6. Pro Advice

Don’t overcrowd pans, let meat rest, use fresh ingredients, and always learn from mistakes.
